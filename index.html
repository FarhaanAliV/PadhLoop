Frequently Asked Questions

PadhLoop is an e-learning platform where users can upload, download, and share study materials. It also allows users to ask and answer questions to facilitate collaborative learning.

After logging in, navigate to the “Upload Material” page. Fill in the title of your material, select the file you wish to upload, and click “Upload” to submit.

You can upload files in common formats such as PDF, DOCX, PPT, and others. Ensure your file is under the maximum allowed size.

Go to the “Upload Material” page, where you’ll find a list of uploaded materials. Click on the title of the material you wish to download.

Navigate to the material you’re interested in and use the comment or discussion section to post your question.
